About Paigos

A family-born barbering name with a professional future

Born and raised in Tshabo Block 2, Nkosekhaya Mavata started cutting hair at 15 after watching his older brother serve clients. Paigos now carries that family legacy into a registered, community-focused barbershop.

The name Paigos comes from a nickname given by his late father, rooted in the idea of sharing fairly. That same spirit shapes the shop: quality cuts, affordable service, and practical training for young people who want to create their own opportunities.
